## How do I reset my sync?

Go to Settings > Sync > Reset Sync. This will clear the local sync index and re-index all files from scratch. Your files on the cloud will not be deleted. The re-indexing process may take 10-30 minutes depending on the number of files.

## What file types are supported?

CloudSync supports all file types. Individual files can be up to 5GB. For files larger than 1GB, we recommend using the desktop client rather than the web uploader.

## How many devices can I sync?

- Free plan: up to 3 devices
- Pro plan: up to 10 devices
- Enterprise plan: unlimited devices

## How do I share a folder with my team?

1. Right-click the folder in CloudSync
2. Select "Share" > "Share with Team"
3. Choose team members or enter email addresses
4. Set permission level: Viewer, Editor, or Admin
5. Click "Share"

Shared folders count toward the storage quota of the folder owner.

## How do I set up auto-backup?

1. Open CloudSync desktop client
2. Go to Settings > Backup
3. Click "Add Backup Folder"
4. Select the local folder to back up
5. Choose backup frequency: Real-time, Hourly, or Daily
6. Click "Enable Backup"

Auto-backup runs in the background and does not require the CloudSync folder to be open.

## What happens when I delete a file?

Deleted files are moved to the CloudSync Trash. They remain there for 30 days (Free/Pro) or 90 days (Enterprise) before permanent deletion. You can restore files from Trash at any time during the retention period.

## How do I enable two-factor authentication?

1. Go to Account Settings > Security
2. Click "Enable 2FA"
3. Choose method: Authenticator App (recommended) or SMS
4. Follow the setup wizard
5. Save your recovery codes in a safe place

2FA is required for all Enterprise accounts.
